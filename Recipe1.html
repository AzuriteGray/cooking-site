Ingredients

  • 200G Small Shrimps
  • 2ML Mayonaise
  • 20G Horse radish
  • 30G Red onion
  • 20G Chives
  • 2 slices Bread
  • Dill
  • Pepper
  • Salt

for 2 persons

Step by step

  1. Wash the shrimp and dry them off
  2. Cut the onion and chives in thin pieces
  3. Grate the horse radish
  4. Mix the shrimp onion chives and horse racish with the mayo
  5. Add pepper and salt to personal taste
  6. Toast the bread put the shrimp mix ontop of it
  7. add the dill for garnish ontop
