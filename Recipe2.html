Ingredients

  • 2 DL Macaroni
  • 2.5 DL creme faiche
  • 2 eggs
  • 100G cheese
  • 250G Chicken
  • 1DL pesto
  • 150G Brocollli
  • Cocktail tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Pesto pasta

for 2 persons

Step by step

  1. Put a pot with water on the stove and bring it to the boil and add the macaroni
  2. Put a second pot with water on the stove for the brocoli
  3. Cut the chicken into pieces of about 1 by 1 cm and fry it in a pan
  4. Mix the eggs creme fraice and cheese in a pot and heat it up
  5. Stir the creme fraice mixture and dont let it become too hot
  6. Mix the creme fraice mixture with the pesto
  7. Take the pasta and brocoli out of the water and mix it with the chicken and sauce
  8. Mix in the cocktail tomatoes for garnish
  9. Add pepper and salt to personal taste
